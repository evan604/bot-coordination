# Marketing Bot Army - Product Pipeline

## Roles

### Dwight (Research)
**Job:** Monitor market for product opportunities
- Check Gumroad trending daily
- Monitor competitor pricing ($29 vs $49 vs $99)
- Track X/LinkedIn hashtags (#OpenClaw, #AIAgents, #NoCode)
- Output: `intel/MARKET-INTEL.md`

### Kelly (X/Twitter)
**Job:** Short-form content, threads, product teasers
- 3-5 tweets per product
- Threads explaining "why this matters"
- Engagement replies
- Output: `marketing/X-DRAFTS.md`

### Rachel (LinkedIn)
**Job:** Professional thought leadership
- Long-form posts about problems solved
- Case study format
- "I built this because..." stories
- Output: `marketing/LINKEDIN-DRAFTS.md`

### Pam (Newsletter)
**Job:** Weekly email digest
- Product launches
- Behind-the-scenes
- Value-first content
- Output: `marketing/NEWSLETTER-DRAFTS.md`

### Haley (Instagram)
**Job:** Visual content
- Product mockups
- Quote cards
- "Day in life" stories
- Output: `marketing/INSTAGRAM-DRAFTS.md`

### Monica (Coordination)
**Job:** Approve and schedule
- Review all drafts
- Select best content
- Schedule via Publer
- Track analytics

## Workflow

**Monday:** Dwight research → Intel file
**Tuesday:** Kelly/Rachel/Haley draft → Content files
**Wednesday:** Monica review → approval
**Thursday:** Schedule in Publer (for next week)
**Friday:** Analytics review → adjust

## Content Types

### Product Launch Sequence
1. **Tease** (7 days before): "Building something..."
2. **Problem** (5 days): "Here's what frustrated me..."
3. **Solution** (3 days): "Here's what I built..."
4. **Launch** (day of): "Live now..."
5. **Social Proof** (3 days after): "First reviews..."

### Evergreen Rotation
- Educational (60%): How-to, tips, insights
- Promotional (20%): Direct product mentions
- Engagement (20%): Questions, polls, shares

## Metrics

Track in `marketing/ANALYTICS.md`:
- Clicks per post
- Gumroad sales
- Email open rates
- Follower growth
